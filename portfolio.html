Space Invaders

'Space Intakers’, a game that was a twist on the original classic Space Invaders, would be what I consider my first official game, made in Java. It was originally an assignment for one of my many first-year CS courses, but I decided it was too good to leave in the past, and I prefer to carry it with me as a symbol of how far I’ve come and learned in my journey to being a game designer and creator.

Nightlight Nightmares

An itch.io game Jam entry made with a team, based on the theme of Defense on the constraint of 'limited resources'. We decided to go with the idea of a girl character protecting her RV camper form evil entities ( a set amount, after which she wins) with here flashlight, which runs down the longer it is kept on. My role was primarily the coding aspect, but it did some concept art too, such as the main character
