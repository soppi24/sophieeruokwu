Game

Nightlight Nightmares

An itch.io game Jam entry made with a team of 2 people, based on the theme of Defense on the constraint of ‘limited resources. We decided to go with the idea of a girl character protecting her RV camper form evil entities ( a set amount, after which she wins) with here flashlight, which runs down the longer it is kept on. My role was primarily the coding aspect, but it did some concept art too, such as the main character
